Mudhara Dongar project

In August 2023, Nisarg Pratisthan planted 1000 diverse saplings on Mudhara Dongar, a hill north of Awasari Khurd Village. An irrigation system, pipelines, and motor pump were installed, with collaborative support from Awasari Khurd villagers and Pune-based Runanubandh Parivar, a nature activist group.

Ramtekdi Project

Nisarg Pratisthan planted 500 diverse saplings in the Ramtekadi area, behind Shree Bhairavnath Vidyalaya Awasari Khurd, amid the Covid-19 pandemic. After three years, the plants have flourished, reaching 5 to 6 feet in height and remaining in excellent condition.

Plantation at the both sides of Roads

In Awasari Khurd, including College Road, Shinde Mala Road, and Smashan Bhumi area, approximately 500 diverse saplings were planted, thriving and now offering shade to passersby. Next year, there are plans to extend the plantation drive along most roads in Awasari Khurd.

Jeevan Mitra Project

Nisarg Pratishtan provides plant saplings to parents at Awasari Khurd PHC center to commemorate childbirth. Similarly, during 'Dashkriya Vidhi' after a person's death, plant saplings are given to family members for plantation with funeral ash, aiming to control river water pollution and enhance plant growth with nutrient-rich ash.

Awasari Khurd Bailgada Ghat

In collaboration with Runanubandh Pariwar, Bhairavnath Vidyalaya Awasari Khurd 1996 Batch, and Nisarg Group (Pune), Nisarg Pratisthan organized a tree plantation drive at Awasari Khurd Bailgada Ghat. With 100 saplings planted, efforts continue for future plantations at Mudhara Dongar near the Engineering College, aiming to establish a tourist destination.

Students Training Programme (STP)

Nisarg Pratisthan organized a two-day student training program, covering topics like communication skills, personality development, environmental conservation, and scientific approach. Various esteemed speakers, including Dr. Shakurao Korde and Nayab Tahsildar Swapnil Khollam, shared insights. The program concluded with a valedictory session and participant feedback.
