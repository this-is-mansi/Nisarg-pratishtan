Nisarg Pratishtan is an NGO established during May 2023 under The societies Registration Act 1826

The main motto of the association is Environmental conservation and educational upliftment with social relevance.

To promote sports education by opening gymnasium and train the students with cultural and traditional sports.

To create digital literacy among students & to promote Technical education.

To promote and support the various educational aspects for girl child students.

It also willing to organize various educational awareness programmes for rural, farmer background students.

To perform various social activities by organizing workshops, Seminars, Blood donation camps, National Integration Camps, Group marriages etc.

Environmental conservation through Tree plantation, Water conservation.

To guide the students regarding advances in education and career opportunities in various fields.

To run various health and Diseases awareness programmes, helping poor and deprived patients.

Environmental protection through pollution control activities, Waste water treatment, Garbage management.

To open hostels, libraries, study centers for poor students.
